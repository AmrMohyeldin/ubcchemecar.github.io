How We Represent You

Student Outreach

Connect with the UBC's most diligent and enthusiastic students. UBC has a community of over 50,000 students and faculty. Your logo will be included in the Department of Chemical Engineering newsletter, on our website and Facebook page, and events posters.

AiChE National Conference

Your logo will also be placed on our car and our competition t-shirts which will be seen at the AIChE National Conference held anually in the US. More than 30 university teams as well as industry professionals across North America will be there.

Where Your Contributions Go

Construction and Machining

We are one of the few student teams in UBC that do not have a dedicated student machine shop and must outsource machining work.

Chemicals and Equipment

We need lab-grade chemicals, lab equipment (beakers, pipettes, rubber gloves and etc.) and electronic components (Arduino, multimeters, lasers etc.)

Travel Expenses

Ideally, we would like our entire team to experience and show off their hard work at the competition but we are constrained by travel expenses.
